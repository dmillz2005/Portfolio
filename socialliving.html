Social Living


Website / Social Media Redesign
Web design - Development - Social Media Marketing

This project involved redesigning a website into a branded, clean design with ease of use that helps convert.

Prospect:
Social Living is a Pittsburgh art and craft brand that I created and run. After a year, I decided to redesign the website along with its social media accounts.

Goal:
Create an exciting new web design for Social Living that focuses on its customers and provides an effortless experience throughout all its channels.

Outcome:
After a new design the overall traffic and social media interactions increased by 75%, the sales conversion rate also increased to 5.82%.

-Research -
I began by researching and performing audits on the website and social media channels, as well as having audits performed by outside sources. Looking into aspects of popular ecommerce retailers helped. I combined the information gathered from this research with techniques of human centered design to create a base starting point to develop navigation, user journeys, wireframes.
Then I created tables to show the websites total item selection. Using that information, I created a basic layout of the item selection and possible menu navigation.

- Wireframes - Prototype -
I started sketching out ideas for the flow and layout of the website with mobile-first design in mind. Starting with the user flow through the website, from the homepage to checkout. Had several users interact and test the sketches to make sure the interface was simple and usable. Working with sketches of the full mobile screens I created wireframes and mockups to match with user flow and data.
Next I started working on the actual website by customizing the wordpress template. Using custom css, html, php and more, I completed the design.

- User Testing -
I used various groups to test and give feedback on the website and checkout process.
After successfully launching the redesigned website, I worked on each of the brands social media channels (facebook, instagram, youtube, pinterest) to create a uniform style and branding across all platforms.
A monthly content publishing schedule was established to increase the presence and interactions via social media.
