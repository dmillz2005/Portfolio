Social Living


Website / Social Media Redesign
Web design - Development - Social Media Marketing

This project involved redesigning a website into a branded, clean design with ease of use that helps convert.

Prospect:
Social Living is a Pittsburgh art and craft brand that I created and run. After a year, I decided to redesign the website along with its social media accounts.

Goal:
Create an exciting new web design for Social Living that focuses on its customers and provides an effortless experience throughout all its channels.

Outcome:
After a new design the overall traffic and social media interactions increased by 75%, the sales conversion rate also increased to 5.82%.
